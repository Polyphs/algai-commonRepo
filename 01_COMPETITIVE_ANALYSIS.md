## Competitive Threats & Mitigation

### Threat 1: **Big Tech Entry**
**Risk:** Google/Apple could add Socratic coaching to built-in apps
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage in Socratic productivity niche
- Privacy moat (Big Tech can't offer local-only due to business model)
- Community/brand loyalty through marine conservation mission

### Threat 2: **Open-Source Clones**
**Risk:** Super Productivity could add Socratic features
**Mitigation:**
- Superior UX/design (Flutter vs. web-based)
- Mobile-first experience (open-source tools are desktop-heavy)
- Unique marine branding creates emotional connection
- Low price ($2-9/mo) makes cloning uneconomical

### Threat 3: **Privacy Tool Expansion**
**Risk:** Lunatask, Obsidian could add ADHD-specific features
**Mitigation:**
- Deep ADHD expertise (competitors would need years to build knowledge)
- Socratic coaching requires AI integration (complex for privacy tools)
- Price advantage maintained
- Marine mission creates loyalty beyond features

### Threat 4: **ADHD App Commoditization**
**Risk:** Market saturation as ADHD awareness grows
**Mitigation:**
- Unique combination of 4 differentiators creates moat
- Freedom→Focus→Work→Review framework trademarked
- Marine conservation story differentiates in crowded market
- Local-only privacy is sustainable competitive advantage
